Founding Partner

Mr. Mbanga Siyomunji

Founding Partner

Mr. Mbanga Siyomunji is a people's person and a meticulous lawyer with immense passion for the law. He is an admitted legal practitioner with rights of appearance in the lower courts, the High Court, and the Supreme Court of Namibia.

He holds BA.ED - MSc (Hons), B. Juris, and LLB qualifications from the University of Namibia and brings broad experience across criminal, labour, civil, and commercial matters.

His work includes criminal law and bail applications, labour disputes before courts and quasi-judicial tribunals, civil litigation, and commercial and corporate advisory work including compliance, governance, company formation, trust formation, and transactional support.
