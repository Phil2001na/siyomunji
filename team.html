Founding Partner

Mr. Mbanga Siyomunji

Founding Partner

Mr. Mbanga Siyomunji is a "people's person" and a meticulous lawyer who has immense passion for law. He is an admitted legal practitioner with right of appearance in lower courts, the High Court, and the Supreme Court of Namibia.

With a distinguished academic background from the University of Namibia, Mr. Siyomunji brings a multi-disciplinary depth to his practice — combining sharp legal acumen with practical insight into the commercial and social realities facing Namibian clients.

His approach is straightforward: understand the client's full situation, apply the law with precision, and deliver outcomes that genuinely move matters forward.
