Practice Areas

What We
Do For You

Siyomunji Law Chambers handles a broad spectrum of matters, providing practical legal advice, representation and transactional support across the following areas of practice.

Criminal Law

  • Consulting and legal advice
  • Litigation in Magistrate's and High Court matters
  • Bail applications
  • Appeals

Conveyancing

  • Property transfers, sub-divisions and consolidations
  • Sectional title development and registration
  • Township development and registration
  • Bond registration, notarial practice and ante-nuptial contracts

Labour Law

  • Dispute resolution in private and statutory institutions
  • Managing industrial action and procedural agreements
  • Training, development and industrial relations support
  • Salary negotiations and chairing disciplinary hearings

Commercial Law

  • Mergers, acquisitions, corporate structures and project finance
  • Commercial drafting, legal opinions and tax-related matters
  • Company law, corporate governance and financing matters
  • International trade, financial recoveries and arbitration

Administration of Estates

  • Drafting wills and trusts
  • Estate administration
  • Estate-related litigation
